A website showcasing the best classwork and ability of Trevor Gray as an MSU student.



Long Version:


TrevorGray.com will show the best classwork and web projects from Trevor Gray's time as a student at MSU and well as internship project he has completed, to better enable potential employers to reach out to Trevor Gray for job and career offers.
